About Our School

Building tomorrow's leaders with quality education

First English Medium School in the District

Welcome to Yardland International School, Chitrakoot's premier English medium educational institution. Established in 2016 by Director Shri Vijay Nigam, our school has been providing quality education with a perfect blend of modern teaching methods and traditional values.

We believe every child is unique and deserves personalized attention. That's why we maintain a strict limit of only 25 students per class, ensuring each student receives the care and guidance they need to excel.

Our school is affiliated with the MP State Board and offers education from Play Group to Class 8, focusing on holistic development of children.
